About The Game!

FLIP ITTO is an interactive aid(game) aiming to help children in recoginizing/identifying objects and things around them. The game incognitively helps teach children. The Answer to the picture lies behind the picture. A hint will be given and the answer can be seen when the card is flipped. The game provokes interest in children with the use of Flip Cards.
